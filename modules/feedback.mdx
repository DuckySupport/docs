---
title: "Feedback"
description: "Ducky's Feedback module lets your members easily give your server feedback through ratings/reviews and suggestions."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Suggestions" titleSize="h2">
    - A user can make a **Suggestion **by using `/suggest`. Members can then Upvote or Downvote your suggestion with the Check and Cross Emojis. Any user who has the **Discord Permission **`Manage Server` will overwrite the votes and Accept or Deny the Suggestion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Feedback" titleSize="h2">
    - A user can leave a feedback message by using `/feedback submit`. A user must select the  User who they are leaving a feedback on, a rating of 1 to 5 stars. A reason for the feedback is optional as well.

    The Feedback embed can also be customized to your likings. The Variables can be found at the Embed Builder.
  </Step>
</Steps>
