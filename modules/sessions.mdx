<Steps>
  <Step title="Sessions Channel" titleSize="h2">
    The Sessions channel is where the following gets sent to:

    - SSU
    - SSD
    - SSU Vote
    - Session Low
    - Session full

    The Embeds of the items listen above can be configured to your liking. 
  </Step>
