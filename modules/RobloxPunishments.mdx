---
title: "Roblox Punishments"
description: "Ducky's Roblox Punishments module allows you to manage and log punishments for your ERLC server."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Roblox Punishment Logs Channel:" titleSize="h2">
    - The **Roblox Punishment Logs Channel **is where in-game moderation logs gets sent to like `!ban @xdd_Recon Mass RDM.`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Roblox Punishment Types" titleSize="h2">
    - The **Roblox Punishment Types ** functionallows you to add custom Punishment types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tempban for Moderators" titleSize="h2">
    - The  **Tempban for Moderators** function can limit the duration of temp bans a Moderator can do, if you leave this open there won't be a limit. For example: The limit is set to 3 Days. If a Moderator attemps to Temp ban a user for 4 days it won't be allowed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
