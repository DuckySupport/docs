Ducky's `Discord Server Status` module allows you to setup stats for `Guild Names, `Guild ID's`, `Guild Member's`, `Guild Bot's`,`Guild Human's`(Server Users),`Guild Boost's`, `Guild Boost Level`, `Guild Role's`, `Guild Text Channel's`, `Guild Voice Channel's`, and `Guild Categories\`. Here you you can learn how to create theses stats and how to use the Varibles.

**Creating a Status Channel**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Getting Your Varible">
    To start creating your Discord Status channel, you need to first get a varible from the varible list.

    <Warning>
      You will require at least 1 Varible to create a channel.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Creating Your Channel">
    Once you have gotton your Varible, you need to head to the **\+ Create Status Channel** dropdown button. Once done, You can put what you want and then post the varible how it is.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Example**: (This is after you got your varible, and you clicked Create Status Channel)\
Guild Name: guild.name | After this is created, it will show up something like **Guild Name: Ducky's Pond**

**Editing a Status Channel**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit Status Channel">
    You need to click the "Edit Status Channel" and select the channel you want to edit, And then you simply put the updated items. You still require at least **1** varible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Removing a Status ChanneL**

All you need to do to delete a status channel, Is click the "Remove Status Channel" and select which channel you wnt deleted.
