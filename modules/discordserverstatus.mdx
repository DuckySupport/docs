Ducky's `Discord Server Status` module allows you to setup stats for `Guild Names`, `Guild IDs`, `Guild Members`, `Guild Bots`, `Guild Humans`(Server Users), `Guild Boosts`, `Guild Boost Level`, `Guild Roles`, `Guild Text Channels`, `Guild Voice Channels`, and `Guild Categories\`. Here you you can learn how to create theses stats and how to use the Variables.

### Creating a Status Channel
<Steps>
  <Step title="Getting Your Variable">
    To start creating your Discord Status channel, you need to first get a variable from the variable list.

    <Warning>
      You will require at least 1 Variable to create a channel.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Creating Your Channel">
    Once you have gotton your Variable, you need to head to the **\+ Create Status Channel** dropdown button. Once done, You can put what you want and then post the variable how it is.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a Status Channel
<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit Status Channel">
    You need to click the "Edit Status Channel" and select the channel you want to edit, And then you simply put the updated items. You still require at least **1** variable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing a Status Channel

If you'd like to delete a status channel click the "Remove Status Channel" and select the channel you'd like deleted.
