This page provides the essential configuration options for Ducky's **Activity Management** module, which provides an easy way to manage and track your staff's activity with LOAs and activity waves. To enable and configure this module, use the **`/setup`** command and navigate to the **Activity Management** page.

## LOAs Enabled

Enabling this option will allow your staff members to request a **Leave of Absence** via the `/loa request` command.

## LOA Logs Channel

The **LOA Logs Channel** is where all LOA requests and logs will be sent. Any user with access to this channel can accept or deny LOA requests.

## LOA Role

The **LOA Role** is assigned to staff members with an active LOA.

## LOA Nickname

The **LOA Nickname** is assigned to staff members with an active LOA.

You may use these variables within the **LOA Nickname**:

- **`{member.name}:`** The name of the member on LOA.
- **`{member.username}:`** The username of the member on LOA.

## Activity Report Interval

The **Activity Report Interval** is the interval where **Activity Reports** are generated, which is the point where Ducky creates a snapshot of all staff member's total shift time and statistics.

You can view Activity Reports via the **`/activity report`** command.
