---
title: "Staff Management"
description: "Ducky's Staff Management module allows for you to infract and promote your staff members."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Infractions Channel" titleSize="h2">
    - The **Infractions Channel** is where the infractions gets sent to. A user can be Infracted when a  **ERLC Manager **runs the `/infraction issue` command.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Promotions Channel" stepNumber={2} titleSize="h2">
    - The **Promotions Channel** is where the Promotions gets sent to. A user can be promoted when a **ERLC Manager **runs the `/promote` command. The Promotions embed can also be customized to your likings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Infraction Types" titleSize="h2">
    - The **Infraction Types** fucntions allows you to add any type of infractions like Warnings, Terminations, etc. The Infraction types also has a Embed Builder which can be used to enchance the Infraction Messages and automatic actions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
