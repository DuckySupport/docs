This page provides the essential configuration options for Ducky's **Server Economy** module, which allows your members to participate in a fun, engaging in-server economy. The module creates a centralized system for collecting and managing **Quacks**, ensuring all transactions and balances are properly tracked. To configure this module, use the `/setup` command and navigate to the **Server Economy** page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Economy Commands" titleSize="h2">
    Ducky has a vast ammount of Commands, these include:

    **`/deposit <amount>`** (**`d!dep`**): Deposit quacks into your bank!\
    **`/withdraw <amount>`** (**`d!wd`**, **`d!with`**): Withdraw quacks from your bank!\
    **`/shop`** (**`d!store)`**: Open the shop.\
    **`/donate <user> <amount>`** (**`d!give`**, **`d!transfer`**): Donate quacks to the given user!\
    **`/balance <?amount>`** (**`d!bal`**, **`d!quacks`**): Show how many quacks you have.\
    **`/work`**: Work to make some quacks!\
    **`/beg`**: Beg for quacks.\
    **`/leaderboard <?type>`** (**`d!lb`**): Show your server's economy leaderboard!\
    **`/slotmachine <bet>`** (**`d!slots`**): Gamble with your quacks in a slot machine.\
    **`/buy <item>`** (**`d!purchase`**): Buy an item from the shop.\
    **`/inventory <?user>`** (**`d!inv`**): View your inventory.\
    **`/rob <rob>`**: Robs the given user!\
    **`/crime`**: Commits a crime!\
    **`/use <item>`**: Use an item in your inventory.\
    **`/daily`**: Claim your daily bonus!\
    **`/blackjack <bet>`** (**`d!bj`**, **`d!bjack`**): Gamble with your quacks in a game of Blackjack.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Custom Replies" titleSize="h2">
    The **Custom Replies** feature allows you to seamlessly create custom replies for the Work, Beg, Rob and Crime commands. You can create any reply, just rember to insert these variables: **`{amount}`** and **`{target}`**. **`{target}`** can only be used for rob command, **`{amount}`** must be used for all custom replies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Custom Cooldowns" titleSize="h2">
    The Edit Custom cooldowns feature is used to set a Cooldown on the commands so that spamming won't occure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
